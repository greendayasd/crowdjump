Thank you for visiting Crowdjump!
In this follow-up study to the well received original Crowdjump you can experience the design progress of a video game and maybe even decide the further development while helping with a student project!
Each study will take about 3 weeks. While the game will evolve daily, you are free to decide when and how often you play yourself!
